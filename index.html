About Me

Hi, my name is Ralph Francesconi. I am a Full Stack Software Engineer trained through General Assembly's Software Engineering Immersive. In 2019, I started my career by attaining a bachelor's degree in Management Information Systems from The University of Massachusetts Boston. This was the first step in my goal of become a software engineer after taking courses about Java, Python, and Visual Basic at the school. Upon graduation I realized that I needed more skills to land a job and thats when General Assembly came in. In this program, I was exposed to many different languages and frameworks. The program emphasized use of fundamentals of Javascript and introduced me to React, Django, Python, SQL and many more. It has inspired my growth to learn different languages to put in my toolbelt. Through my journey I have developed a growth mindset, and I am excited to expand my knowledge on my way to becoming a leader in the field of technology.
