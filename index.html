Anand Ramanathan, Co-founder

Anand has over 15 years of experience in AI and data science, primarily focussing on natural language processing and text analytics. He has developed analytics solutions for varied domains including finance, language solutions, telecom and oil & gas, while working with organizations such as the National Centre for Software Technology, IBM Research, and Schlumberger. His work comprises research in machine translation, an information extraction and search product for financial data analytics, an open source intelligence toolkit, a ticket analytics system, and various other NLP solutions. Anand has a PhD in computer science from IIT Bombay.

Vivek Mehta, Co-founder

Vivek has 15+ years of AI/ML experience. For the last 2 years he has worked on startups related to conversational discovery and behavioural authentication. Before starting his entrepreneurial journey, he worked as a Data Scientist at Flipkart. In five years at Flipkart, he worked on problems related to personalization and recommendations, fraud detection and a few other. Before Flipkart, Vivek worked in several different startups and research labs. He has worked on an array of domains like demand forecasting, online advertising, handwriting recognition, machine translation, and gesture recognition. Vivek holds a masters degree from Carnegie Mellon University.
